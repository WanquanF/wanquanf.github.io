Wanquan Feng (冯万泉)

I am currently a researcher at ByteDance, working on exciting projects related to 2D/3D AIGC, 3D vision, and digital human. I served as a researcher at Y-tech, Kuaishou Technology (Kwai Inc.) from July 2022 to November 2023. I earned my Ph.D. degree in 2022 from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), under the guidance of Prof. Juyong Zhang. Prior to my doctorate, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Statistics from USTC in 2016.
