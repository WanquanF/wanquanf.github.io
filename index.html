Short Bio

I am currently a researcher at ByteDance, working on exciting projects related to generative AI, 2D/3D vision, and digital human. I served as a researcher at Y-tech, Kuaishou Technology (Kwai Inc.) from July 2022 to November 2023. I earned my Ph.D. degree in 2022 from School of Mathematical Sciences at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), under the guidance of Prof. Juyong Zhang. Prior to my doctorate, I obtained my bachelor's degree from USTC in 2016.

NOTE: As of 2025, my research interests primarily focus on VIDEO GENERATION and 4D SCENE GENERATION. I welcome discussions on related topics; let's collaborate and strive for progress together (✿◠‿◠)
